# Demo Scenarios

Try these commands to test the capabilities of the agent:

## 1. Tone Change
> "Rewrite the first paragraph in a much more friendly and humorous tone."

## 2. Summarization
> "Add a short conclusion section at the end summarizing all the points above."

## 3. Formatting
> "Convert the second paragraph into a bulleted list."

## 4. Expansion
> "Elaborate on the introduction section, mentioning the importance of cybersecurity."

## 5. Question Answering (Chat)
> "Do you think there are any important points missing from this article?"
